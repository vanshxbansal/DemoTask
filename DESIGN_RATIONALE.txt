सूर्यपुरा ग्राम विकास पोर्टल — Design Rationale (Skill Assessment Demo)
================================================================================

FORM FIELD — Short explanation (copy-paste ready, ~150 words)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

मैंने यह डिज़ाइन इसलिए बनाया क्योंकि ग्राम विकास पोर्टल को भरोसे और भावना दोनों
चाहिए। Hero में राजेश वर्मा (ग्राम विकास राजदूत) से उपयोगकर्ता तुरंत जुड़ता है।
पाँच pillar cards — शिक्षा, किसान, सड़क, पंचायत, डिजिटल पहचान — brief के theme
को सीधे दिखाते हैं। Saffron, forest green और cream palette गर्म, premium और rural
feel देती है, सस्ते सरकारी टेम्पलेट जैसी नहीं। Mobile-first layout, बड़े Hindi
फ़ॉन्ट (Noto Sans Devanagari) और 44px+ tap targets ग्रामीण उपयोगकर्ताओं के लिए
सुविधाजनक हैं। Impact stats और initiatives trust बनाते हैं। Footer में demo
disclaimer है — सभी नाम/आंकड़े काल्पनिक हैं।

I chose this design because a rural development portal must feel trustworthy and
emotional. The hero leader humanizes the site; five pillar cards map directly to
the brief themes. Warm saffron/green/cream palette feels premium, not generic
gov-template. Mobile-first Hindi typography and large touch targets suit village
users. Stats and initiative cards build credibility. Footer labels all content as
fictional demo material.


FULL RATIONALE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Emotional trust
   - Hero character (visionary rural leader) creates human connection
   - Leader quote + portrait in spotlight section reinforce authenticity
   - Impact statistics (98% attendance, ₹2.4Cr farmer aid, 24/7 helpline) prove outcomes

2. Theme clarity (assessment brief alignment)
   - Five pillars explicitly cover: शिक्षा, किसान, सड़क, पंचायत, डिजिटल पहचान
   - Initiatives section shows real-world programs (mid-day meal tracking, Kisan helpline, e-Panchayat)
   - Dual CTAs: explore schemes + create digital identity

3. Premium rural aesthetic
   - Avoided cold blue/gray government clichés
   - Warm earth tones (saffron #E67E22, forest #2D6A4F, terracotta #C1440E, cream #FFFBF5)
   - Soft shadows, rounded-2xl cards, generous whitespace
   - Unsplash photography: fields, schools, panchayat — grounded imagery

4. Mobile-first UX
   - Stacked grid on small screens, 2-col on tablet, 3-col on desktop
   - Hamburger nav with full-width mobile menu
   - Base font 16px+, Hindi line-height 1.6+ for readability
   - Sticky nav with backdrop blur for modern feel

5. Social & export consistency
   - Same design tokens on /export/hero, /export/post1, /export/post2
   - Post 1: sunrise narrative "सूर्यपुरा में नया सूर्योदय"
   - Post 2: farmer empowerment with helpline CTA
   - Ensures brand consistency between website and social assets

6. Demo safety
   - Footer disclaimer: fictional names, photos, content for assessment only
   - Registration CTA disabled with "Demo" label
   - English labels sparingly (Visionary Rural Leader, Export Assets) for assessor clarity
